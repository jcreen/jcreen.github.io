Education

Bachelor of Arts in Elementary Education

Philosophy of Education

I believe each and every child has the potential to bring something unique and special to the world. All children are lifelong learners who need a secure, caring, and stimulating environment in which they can reach their fullest potential. Education is the window of opportunity for students to explore their personal interest and improve their wealth of knowledge.

Personal Statement

This is my 14th year in education and I am still excited about learning and teaching. There is nothing more rewarding than setting goals with students, parents, colleagues and working collaboratively to reach them. I am indebted to my elementary school teachers for cultivating my desire to learn and feel and obligation to pay it forward with my students. I obtained my Level 1 Google Educator Certification in September of 2016 and have attended several technology focused conferences.
